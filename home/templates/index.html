What is this website for?

This web app uses DeepLearning to predict if a human being has Pneumonia by analyzing their X-Ray. A normal chest would contain no abnormal opacification in the X-RAY image, lungs with bacterial pneumonia will exhibit a focal lobar consolidation and lungs with viral pneumonia manifests with a more diffuse interstital pattern in both lungs.
